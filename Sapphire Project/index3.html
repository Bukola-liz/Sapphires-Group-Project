ABOUT THE EVENT

The cake Menia show is an annual cake event that gives young enterpreneurs - not just cake bakers - the opportunityand platform to sell themselves and thier brand to a large and receptive audience. CakeMenia incorporates a live cake decoration competition where winners go home with cash prices, brand new mixers and ovens, it also ahs top bakers from all over Nigeria come to exhibit masterpieces. The cake Menia 'Sugar Wars' contest features bakers with skills that transcends the basic and ordinary, all competing to come out on top and be recognized as the best in the business. The contest features cake gore, speed, artistic creativity , and lots of sugar.
